Archies on the Creek

Archies on the Creek, was a local restaurant, which featured extravagent architecture and sat next to a lake. The venue was used to host weddings. Upon it's closure in 2012, the facilities remained unused until it was recently used by Adverto, a company which creates digital advertisement displays for fuel pumps.
