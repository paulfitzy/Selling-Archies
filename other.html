Archies Creek

Archies Creek features many OTHER significant landmarks, these include but aren't limited to:
- The Local Bus stop
- The Old Butter Factory (Est Late 1800's)
- The Timber Factory
- The Creek in which the town got it's name
These landmarks shape the town, turning what would otherwise be a small community into the township that is Archies Creek and although the town is small, there is a lot of history to explore.
