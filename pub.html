Archies Creek Hotel and Pub

The Archies Creek Hotel and Pub was one of the first business's to be built in the town. The pub was founded in 1902 when Mrs Mary A Dixon applied for a hotel license. The Pub is now an epicentre in the town, hosting many musical talents and providing a good meal to those visiting from the surrounding towns.
