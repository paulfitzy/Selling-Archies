Archies Creek Town Hall

Archies Creek Town Hall was opened on the 1st of January 1904. The hall hosted many balls and dances for the local towns people, these were held during full moons to provide light for the men on horses to find their way home. The hall is still used to hold events going on around the town and to raise money for the local community.
